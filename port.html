In 2025, I watched my parents struggle — manual ledgers, forgotten debts, zero visibility into their business finances. Instead of accepting that as normal, I built them a solution. #01 · FEATURED
AgriSense
Analytics Engineer · React · IoT · Python · MySQL
The Problem
Rwandan farmers lose income every season through guesswork — planting wrong crops, over-investing in inputs, with zero data about their own soil. No affordable intelligence tool existed for smallholder farmers.
How I Solved It
Built an IoT pipeline connecting soil sensors to a React dashboard. Conducted on-site farm visits and partnered with FAO to validate the model. Real farmers tested it in the field before launch.
70%+ prediction accuracy on first deployment · FAO validated · Real farmer deployment
