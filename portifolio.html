01 · ABOUT ME

The person behind
the code.

In 2025, I watched my parents struggle — manual ledgers, forgotten debts, zero visibility into their business finances. Instead of accepting that as normal, I built them a solution. That was my first real project, and it changed how I think about software: it must solve real problems for real people.

Now I'm at Rwanda Coding Academy — one of East Africa's most selective tech institutions — studying Software Programming and Embedded Systems. I've since built AgriSense, an IoT-powered crop recommendation platform that achieved 70%+ accuracy on its first deployment.

My long-term ambition is to merge software engineering with avionics — building the systems that will power Rwanda's aviation future. Until then, I'm shipping code, building my portfolio, and looking for my next challenge.
